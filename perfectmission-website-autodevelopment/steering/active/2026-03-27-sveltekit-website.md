# Task

## Goal

Build a production-quality SvelteKit website for Perfect Mission Ltd — an AI-driven real estate development consultancy operating across emerging markets in CEE, Latin America, and global frontier markets.

## Context

Perfect Mission Ltd (UK company #08651715, registered at 20 Wenlock Road, London N1 7GU) is being activated as an AI-driven real estate consultancy. The company's edge is speed: AI-powered analysis, decision-making, financing, and execution — built on 30 years of founder expertise (Matej Havlin and Lukas Havel).

**Domains:** perfectmission.co.uk (primary) + perfectmission.uk (redirect)
**Stack:** SvelteKit (TypeScript)
**Hosting:** Docker (httpd:2.4-alpine) behind Traefik on local Mac, deployed via github/websites/ docker-compose.yml
**Design:** See `steering/design-language.md` for the full design specification (generated by Advisory Council)

**Markets covered:**
- CEE: Czech Republic, Bulgaria, Romania, Poland, Moldova, Albania
- LATAM: Honduras, Dominican Republic, Bolivia, Panama, Belize, Cuba
- Global: Vietnam, Morocco

**Target audience:** Institutional investors, family offices, real estate developers looking for emerging market opportunities.
